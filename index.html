About Us

RaffleBot was made out of frustration for a lack of simple, user-friendly, and free raffle-drawing options for Twitter. This tool was made by Izzy (ZazSaysStuff) and Charlie (Hatchl) using Github, Twitter's API, Python, and a free bootstrap template.
